About the Shop

Under-the-radar, and proud of it.

Mobile Miramar Auto Detailing is a small, independent detailing operation working out of the back of the Mobil gas station on Miramar Road near the I-15. No storefront, no big marketing budget, just careful, hands-on detail work and a loyal base of neighborhood customers.

Reviewers consistently praise the thorough, meticulous approach and reasonable prices. Customers say their cars “look and feel so different” after a visit. It's the kind of operator you find through word of mouth, and once you find it, you stick with it.
